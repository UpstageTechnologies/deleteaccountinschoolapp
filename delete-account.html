Account Deletion Request

This page explains how users can request deletion of their account and associated personal data for applications developed by Upstage Technologies.

📚 Upstage Schools

If you would like to permanently delete your Upstage Schools account and associated data, please follow the steps below.

How to request account deletion

  1. Send an email to upstagetechnologies@gmail.com.
  2. Use the subject: Delete My Upstage Schools Account.
  3. Include your registered email address, school name (if applicable), and registered mobile number.
  4. Our support team will verify your request.
  5. Your account and associated personal data will be permanently deleted within 48 hours.

Data that will be deleted

  • User Profile
  • Student Records
  • Teacher Records
  • Parent Records
  • Attendance Records
  • Messages
  • Assignments
  • Uploaded Images
  • Other personal information associated with the account

Certain financial or legal records may be retained where required by applicable law.

🛒 Kadai

If you would like to permanently delete your Kadai account and associated personal data, please follow the steps below.

How to request account deletion

  1. Send an email to upstagetechnologies@gmail.com.
  2. Use the subject: Delete My Kadai Account.
  3. Include your registered email address or registered mobile number.
  4. Our support team will verify your request.
  5. Your account and associated personal data will be permanently deleted within 48 hours.

Data that will be deleted

  • User Account
  • Profile Information
  • Saved Addresses
  • Order History
  • Wishlist
  • Shopping Cart
  • Uploaded Profile Images
  • Other personal information associated with the account

Certain transaction records or information required to comply with applicable laws may be retained for the period required by law. All remaining personal data will be permanently deleted.
