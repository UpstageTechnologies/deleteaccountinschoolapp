Delete Your Upstage Schools Account

If you would like to delete your Upstage Schools account and associated data, please follow these steps.

How to request account deletion

  1. Login to Upstage Schools.
  2. Go to Dashboard → Accounts.
  3. Select Delete Account.
  4. Confirm your request.

If you cannot access your account, email us at:

upstagetechnologies@upstageindia.com

Data that will be deleted

  • User Profile
  • Student Records
  • Teacher Records
  • Parent Records
  • Attendance
  • Messages
  • Assignments
  • Uploaded Images

Data that may be retained

Certain financial or legal records may be retained where required by applicable law.
